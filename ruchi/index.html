ABOUT THE HOTEL

Located in the Mysore, it enjoys proximity to all places of tourist and commercial interest in and around Mysore. Hotel Ruchi offers a range of modern facilities and services at reasonable prices. With accommodation options for all budgets, the hotel is ideal for business travelers as well as leisure travelers. Ruchi the prince is a premium hotel offering contemporary services and facilities in a sassy ambience. The hotel offers massage service on request. Other facilities available at Hotel Ruchi the prince include a car rental service, safety deposit lockers, laundry & dry cleaning service, doctor on call and spacious parking. Outstanding location and the simple yet comfortable rooms mark this leisure/business hotel out from the crowd outside. With a elegant and glossy exterior hotel Ruchi offers all the facilities and services to ensure that its guests have a comfortable stay while in the city.
